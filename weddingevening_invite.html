Details

At 7pm on Saturday 28th October at Llanhilleth Miners Intitute we will be kicking off our evening do to celebrate our marriage. There is a car park next to the building and the main entrance is on the road. The main hall is situated up the stairs found in the entrance and the bar above the top table.

We will be serving chip rolls at around 9pm and the venue will close at 12:00am



Directions

For those with SatNav just enter the details in below. The Miners Institute is signposted from the main road. Short URL for Google Maps http://bit.ly/2vjw2zf

Llanhilleth Miners Institute Limited
Meadow Street
Llanhilleth
Nr Abertillery
Blaenau Gwent
NP13 2JT

Travelling from Abergavenny or if you pass Abergavenny, head up the Heads of the Valleys (A465) towards Brynmawr. Go straight over all temporary roundabouts until the Brynmawr roundabout (the 2nd major roundabout) and take the 1st exit signposted for Abertillery. At the next roundabout take the 3rd exit towards Abertillery, Nantyglo and at the following roundabout take the 2nd exit towards Abertillery. Continue past Abertillery and at the next roundabout take the 2nd exit towards Newport. Continue until the next set of traffic lights and turn left into Llanhilleth. Follow the road around to the left and the Miners Institute will be just on the left. It is signposted from the main road.

Travelling from all other routes using the M4, exit at junction 27 and take the Brynmawr, Risca (A467) exit. Follow all signs to Brynmawr (A467), continue on the A467 past the traffic lights at Crumlin, this is an offset cross junction with 2 sets of traffic lights to go through. Once past the lights continue on to the next set of traffic lights and turn right into Llanhilleth. Follow the road around to the left and the Miners Institute will be just on the left. It is signposted from the main road.

And appreciated by us,
